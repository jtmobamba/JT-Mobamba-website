Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

Is the desktop app free?

Yes! The JT Mobamba desktop app is completely free to download and use. Shop the same products at the same prices as our website.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure connections. Your payment information is never stored locally on your device.

What's the difference between installer and portable versions?

The installer version integrates with Windows (Start Menu, auto-updates). The portable version can run from any folder or USB drive without installation.

Can I use my existing account?

Yes! Sign in with the same credentials you use on our website. Your cart, wishlist, and order history will sync automatically.

Will there be a Mac or Linux version?

We're working on it! Mac and Linux versions are in development. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when they're available.
