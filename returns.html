Return Policy

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging with all accessories and manuals
  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date
  • Accompanied by the receipt or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items with broken seals on software or digital products
  • Items that have been used or damaged by the customer
  • Gift cards and downloadable software
  • Items purchased on final sale

Refund Process

Once we receive your return:

  • We will inspect the item within 2 business days
  • You will receive an email notification of the refund status
  • If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • Refunds typically appear within 5-7 business days
