Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days delivery. Free shipping is available on all orders over $50.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Do you offer warranty on products?

Yes! All products come with a comprehensive 2-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available for purchase at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also visit our Track Order page to check your order status.
